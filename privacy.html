Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/1/2025

Trail Post (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Trail Post mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: Account information is only stored on your device and not saved on our servers.

User Content: As an optional feature, you can automatically submit your blog to be shown at trailpost.io

b. Automatically Collected Information

Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as its operating system, unique device identifier, and browser type.

Usage Information: Details about how you use the App, including pages viewed, features used, and interaction times.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • Providing and maintaining the App’s functionality.
  • Enhancing user experience and personalizing features.
  • Sending you updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Conducting research and analytics to improve the App.
  • Ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: To perform App-related services such as hosting, analytics, and customer support.
  • For Legal Compliance: If required to do so by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Trail Post, our users, or others.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your information.

4. Your Choices

  • Account Information: You can update or delete your account information in the App settings.
  • Location Services: You can disable location services in your device settings.
  • Promotional Communications: You can opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link in those emails.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the App’s services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When your information is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it.

6. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

8. International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in a country where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be effective when posted in the App. Your continued use of the App signifies your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
